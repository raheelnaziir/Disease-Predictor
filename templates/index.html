This tool uses a Neural Network + Random Forest Ensemble trained on 41 diseases and ~130 symptoms, with NLP-based symptom extraction from free text.


Built with Python · Flask · TensorFlow · scikit-learn · HTML/CSS/JS


For educational purposes only. Not a substitute for professional medical advice.

How to use

  • Type your symptoms in plain English in the input box
  • Or click the 📎 paperclip to upload a .txt or .pdf file
  • Or click the 🎤 mic to speak your symptoms
  • Press send or hit Enter to get your prediction

Tips

  • Be descriptive — mention at least 3–4 symptoms
  • Example: "I have been feeling dizzy and tired with a headache and frequent urination"
  • The confidence bar shows how certain the model is

Always consult a real doctor for medical decisions.
